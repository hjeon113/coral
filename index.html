Coral Layered Memory
Time in Section
i
Concept
A sculptural archive of time inscribed in coral skeletons. Corals grow annually, recording density shifts layer by layer within their structure. Each species' unique growth path is translated into a 3D ribbon sculpture, encoding density data from Hainan Island (1976–2003). OHP film is then applied to the ribbon surface, visualising the change in density that marks 1982 as a turning point.
